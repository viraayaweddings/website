Wedding Service Payment Plan

Wedding Planning Fee

At The Wedding Company, the word 'planning' covers every aspect towards making your wedding day come alive perfectly, including execution. This includes scouting suitable venues, envisioning the decor, and overseeing minute details of your big day. You decide the level of support you need–whether it's specific venue requirements or complete end-to-end wedding planning services.

Since each project (no matter how niche) requires dedicated time, expert industry contacts, careful coordination, and on-the-ground support, we charge a Planning Fee.

This fee allows us to:

Build the right team dedicated to your celebration

Leverage our vendor network to secure the best deals for you

Provide on-ground support from start to finish

In short, the Planning Fee enables us to craft a wedding experience that's uniquely yours and flawlessly executed with the unparalleled services we're known for.

Booking Fee

The first step towards your wedding journey with us.

This is NOT an additional cost — it's the part of your total Wedding Planning Fee.

It allows us to assign you the dedicated Wedding Experience Manager who will consult and plan the wedding, especially curated for you.

It gets your wedding journey officially started with us!
