Luxera Hotel on MG Road, Delhi


Luxera Hotel on MG Road stands as a luminous sanctuary where the charm of a starlit Glass House meets the grandeur of expansive ballrooms. This premium hotel offers a soulful setting for your union, featuring elegant indoor spaces and lush lawns for traditional rituals. As a distinguished wedding venue in Delhi, it provides 15 guest rooms for a restful stay. You have the flexibility to hire outside caterers, decorators, and DJs to personify every festive ceremony near the Arjan Garh Metro.
 

Luxera Hotel Venue Details


  • Location: MG Road, Delhi 
  • Guest Capacity: <50 - 1000
  • Price Range: ₹2500 per plate (veg) | ₹2700 per plate (non-veg)
  • Venue Spaces: Indoor + Outdoor 
  • Rooms: 15 rooms 
  • Parking: Available 
  • Small Function Size: Less than 50 PAX allowed 
  • Decor: Outside decorators permitted 
  • Alcohol: In-house and outside alcohol permitted 
  • Music & DJs: In-house and outside DJ available 
  • Catering: In-house and outside catering allowed 
  • Late Night: Late-night music not allowed
